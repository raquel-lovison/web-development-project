So, Who am I?




My name is Raquel, and I was born and raised in Brazil. I love being outdoors, whether snowboarding or exploring new hiking trails. I have a passsion for travel, which allows me learn about different cultures and try a variety of cuisines from around the world. My curiosity and love for new experience drive me to keep learning and exploring!

I have a background in healthcare, where I developed a strong foundation in problem-solving and attention to detail. While in Brazil, I also worked as a photographer, capturing the beauty of everyday moments and refining my creative perspective. These experiences have taught me to appreciate both the technical and artistic sides of any field. My journey eventually led me into IT, where I discovered a passion for cybersecurity.

As I continue advancing my career in IT and cybersecurity, I'm focused on leveraging my technical skills and experiences to make a meaningful impact in digital security. I'm expanding my knowledge and seeking opportunities to combine my passion for technology with practical problem-solving.
